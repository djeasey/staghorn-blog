Copyright Information & Intellectual Property

Whilst I'm yet to dive into reading about intellectual property law, let me take a swing at this.

This blog was made with the intention of being a catalogue of notes on my many fleeting academic interests. In light of this, information presented in articles may not be cited or sourced at times. This is typically because the information is so prevalent and disseminated in the relevant academic field, that referencing specific sources would solely be a display of ostensible academic value. Academic value isn't what we're about here. Just fun facts about stuff you may not know, that might make you wanna Google some more info.

I seek to present no information more in-depth or specific than could be found on my one true love, Wikipedia. Thus all written content in articles presented here (including medical information) is intended as a dissemination of personal study and interest notes rather than my own intellectual property or professional medical opinion.

However, where possible, I do reference and/or cite all quotations, publications referred to, and images (creative commons and public domain) not created entirely by my own hand.

As is my understanding, the blog has been published in-keeping with standards of fair-use. The central justification for this usage is use for nonprofit-educational purposes. If you should feel in anyway feel that the fair-use principle has been violated, or should you take issue with usage or inadequate crediting of property owners, please do not hesitate to contact me at: staghornblog@gmail.com.
